Sign in with Facebook

This option allows you to use all the features of the Pracuj.pl app without creating an account (registering).

How to access the “sign in with Facebook” screen?

1. From the registration screen: see Register .

Accept the Terms and Conditions (red tick). Tap the Facebook icon.

2. From the sign in screen: see Sign in with your account.

Tap Zaloguj się Facebookiem sign in with Facebook.

 

How to sign in with Facebook?

Follow the steps from 1 or 2 above.

In the window that appears, enter your Facebook credentials: e-mail or phone number and your Facebook password.

Tap Enter.

Facebook will ask for your permission to use cookies stored on the Pracuj.pl application. Tap Allow.

Pracuj.pl will ask for access to your Facebook data: your name, profile picture and e-mail address. Tap Continue as [your name].

 

You are now signed in with Facebook. The application will remember your credentials and you won’t have to sign in again.
