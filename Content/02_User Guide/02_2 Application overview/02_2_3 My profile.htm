My profile

You can find Mój profil my profile under Więcej more : the three little dots icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.

Let’s look at the information that you can complete in your profile.

You don't have to complete any of this information if you prefer to add your CV manually.

On the left, you can see the all the options in Polish, and on the right: in English.

 

1. Photo

Tap the pencil icon. Choose the source of the photo:

  • galeria gallery: choose the photo from your phone gallery,

  • aparat camera: take a photo and set it as your profile picture.

2. Personal data

Here you can fill in your: first and last name, position, place of residence, e-mail (it will be filled in automatically when you’re logged in), phone number and date of brith. When you finish, tap Zapisz save.

3. Expectations

The following options are available:

  • polecanie profilu profile recommendation : turn on if you want recruiters to have access to your personal data,

  • nie polecaj mnie firmom don't recommend me to companies: if you don’t want some specific companies to have access to your profile, tap the plus icon, type the company name, choose it from the list that appears and tap Wybierz select,

  • praca zdalna remote work: turn on if you are open to remote job opportunities,

  • wynagrodzenie salary: type your salary expectations,

  • lokalizacja location: type the desired location of your future job.

Tap Zapisz save.

4. Professional summary

Tap the plus icon. Complete the fields:

  • aktualna pozycja zawodowa current professional position,

  • podsumowanie zawodowe professional summary: for a brief description of yourself as an employee. Tap Zapisz save.

 

5. Professional experience

Tap the plus icon and complete the fields:

  • stanowisko position,

  • lokalizacja location,

  • firma company,

  • data rozpoczęcia start date,

  • nadal pracuję still working: turn on if this is your current position; otherwise, turn it off and complete the field data zakończenia end date,

  • opis stanowiska job description.

Tap Zapisz save. To add another position, tap the plus icon and repeat the steps listed in the bullet points above.

6. Education

Tap the plus icon and complete the fields:

  • nazwa szkoły school name ,

  • poziom wykształcenia level of education,

  • kierunek field,

  • specjalizacja specialization,

  • data rozpoczęcia start date,

  • nadal się uczę still learning: turn on if this is your current school; otherwise, turn it off and complete the field data zakończenia end date.

Tap Zapisz save. To add another school, tap the plus icon and repeat the steps listed in the bullet points above.

7. Skills

Tap the pencil icon. Type your skill. Choose it from the list that appears and tap Zapisz save.

8. Courses, trainings, certificates

Tap the plus icon. Complete the fields: nazwa name, organizator organizer and data date of your course, training or certification. Tap Zapisz save. To add another course, training or certification, tap the plus icon and repeat these steps.

9. Organizations, activities, associations

Tap the plus icon and complete the fields:

  • nazwa name,

  • miejsce location,

  • data rozpoczęcia start date,

  • trwa nadal still going on: turn on if this activity is still going on; otherwise, turn it off and complete the field data zakończenia end date.

  • opis description - description.

Tap Zapisz save. To add another organization, activity or association, tap the plus icon and repeat the steps listed in the bullet points above.

10. Languages

Tap the pencil icon and choose one or more languages from the list. Tap Zapisz save.

11. Hobby

Tap the pencil icon and type your hobbies. Tap Zapisz save.

12. Links

Tap the plus icon. Complete the fields: adres URL URL and typ linku link type. Tap Zapisz save. To add another link, tap the plus icon and repeat these steps.

 

! To edit any of this information, find it under the relevant name (personal data, expectations, education, etc.) on the drop-down list. Tap the little pencil icon. Edit the information and tap Zapisz save.

 

13. Generate CV from profile

The last step is to generate a résumé from the information completed in your profile. For this feature to work, you need to fill in your place of residence (1), professional experience (5) and education (6).Tap the button and your résumé is ready!
