Welcome!

Are you a foreigner looking for a job in Poland? If you are new here or still have problems with the Polish language (understandable, as it is one of the hardest in the world!), you might be searching for work on international platforms, such as LinkedIn or Glassdoor.

However, it is always a good idea to go local. That way, you can broaden your search and increase your chances of landing a better position. This user-friendly guide will help you to quickly download, install and use the application provided by Pracuj.pl – one of the biggest job search platforms in Poland.

The guide is divided into two parts:

1. Application Quickstart Guide

You will learn how to download, install and discover the basic features of the Pracuj.pl app. Without creating an account, you will be able to search for jobs by:

  • key word,

  • location,

  • category.

2. User Guide

You will learn to use all the features that the application has to offer. Registering, logging in with Facebook or with your fingerprint allows you to:

  • save job offers and searches,

  • apply for jobs,

  • create and upload your own documents, such as CVs and cover letters,

  • manage notifications.

 

For any terminological issues, simply tap or hover over any word in this color here is your translation! and an English translation will appear.
